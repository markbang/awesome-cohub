# Home as dumping ground

## Why it hurts

Home becomes an unsearchable junk Space: half projects, half config, zero Saves worth restoring.

## Do this instead

- One Space per job (or per knowledge domain)  
- `name=config` for defaults  
- Labels + Sessions inbox for navigation  

## Smell test

You open Home and do not know what is safe to delete.
